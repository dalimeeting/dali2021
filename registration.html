Please note: DALI is an invitation-only event, and it is not possible to transfer this invitation to others.

The registration fee is 450 € for attendees coming from academia and 600 € for those coming from industry. Registration includes welcome dinner, symposium and workshops, coffee breaks, and a conference dinner (possibly more).

Click here to register for DALI 2021!

You will also be able to reserve a hotel room at the conference venue during the registration process. If you rather want to book your accommodation separately at a later point in time, you can do that here.

In case you book your accommodation before you register for the conference, you will later only have to fill in the personal area in order to select the registration fee. It is not necessary to fill out the registration form again (that way your profile would be duplicated).
